About Me

Hello, I'm Prabesh Rai, a dedicated data enthusiast with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Software Engineering and a Postgraduate degree in Business Analytics from Lambton College. Alongside my academic pursuits, I've gained hands-on experience in software development, particularly as a Flutter Mobile app developer at Yellow Nepal Pvt. Ltd. During my tenure, I translated designs and wireframes into high-quality code, leveraging Flutter to build advanced applications across multiple platforms. Working closely with design teams, I collaborated to ensure seamless integration of user interfaces and functionalities. Throughout my academic journey and professional endeavors, I've delved deep into the world of data analytics, leveraging my expertise to undertake several projects in the realm of big data. From developing predictive models to analyzing vast datasets, I've utilized a variety of platforms and programming languages to tackle real-world challenges. With hands-on experience across diverse big data technologies and methodologies, I'm dedicated to sharing insights and discoveries from my journey through this portfolio. Join me as we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of big data and uncover its transformative potential together.
